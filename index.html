Michael Sully

My work explores the relationship between Bauhausian sensibilities and midlife subcultures. With influences as diverse as Munch and Roy Lichtenstein, new variations are generated from both traditional and modern narratives.

Ever since I was a student I have been fascinated by the theoretical limits of the universe. What starts out as vision soon becomes debased into a cacophony of defeat, leaving only a sense of chaos and the possibility of a new synthesis.

As spatial replicas become reconfigured through diligent and personal practice, the viewer is left with a hymn to the inaccuracies of our future.
